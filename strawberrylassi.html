Strawberry Lassi

A Taste of Sweetness & Health

Our Strawberry Lassi is a refreshing blend of creamy yogurt and ripe strawberries. It's not just a delicious drink; it’s a wholesome treat made with simple, natural ingredients.

Positive Health Impacts

  • Rich in Vitamin C: Strawberries are packed with Vitamin C, which is crucial for a strong immune system and healthy skin.
  • Good for Digestion: The yogurt in lassi is a natural probiotic, promoting a healthy gut and aiding in digestion.
  • Hydrating & Energizing: A perfect beverage to cool down on a hot day, it provides instant hydration and a boost of energy from natural sugars.
  • Supports Bone Health: Lassi is an excellent source of calcium, which is vital for maintaining strong bones and teeth.

Traditional Method: How We Make It

At Lassi Valley, we believe in the traditional method to ensure the most authentic taste and quality. Here’s a simple guide to our process:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: We start with fresh, chilled yogurt and sweet, juicy strawberries. A touch of sugar is added to enhance the flavor.
  2. Churning, Not Blending: We use a traditional wooden churner (or a simple whisk) to gently mix the ingredients. This method retains the creamy, smooth texture of the yogurt, unlike high-speed blenders that can make it frothy.
  3. The Perfect Consistency: A splash of cold water or milk is added to achieve the perfect, drinkable consistency—not too thick, not too thin.
  4. Serve Chilled: The lassi is poured into a glass and often garnished with a fresh strawberry slice. The result is a simple, refreshing, and delicious drink that embodies homemade goodness.
